Welcome to ALm56an Server

Our server, ALm56an, was created in October 2022 as a space for gamers to connect, share experiences, and dive into some of our favorite multiplayer games. We’ve built a fun and collaborative environment where players can enjoy a variety of games like Overwatch, Call of Duty (COD), Dead by Daylight (DBD), and Minecraft.

Games We Play

Overwatch

Overwatch is one of our community’s top choices for team-based action. Whether it's competitive or casual matches, our players enjoy the diverse heroes and fast-paced strategies the game offers.

Call of Duty (COD)

For FPS lovers, Call of Duty is a favorite, with modes like Warzone and team multiplayer bringing exciting and intense action to the server.

Dead by Daylight (DBD)

In this 4v1 horror game, players can either work together to survive or play as the killer in thrilling matches that always keep the tension high.

Minecraft

Minecraft has been a staple for creativity and collaboration on our server. Whether it's building massive structures or tackling survival challenges, it’s a game that brings everyone together.
